Stop Motion Animation

Inception - 1890s

Subjects in stop motion are physical objects moved between every camera exposure. Objects may be armatured with wire or machined metal skeletons, or un-armatured. Stop motion is considerably more ephemeral than other forms of animation because iterative takes require starting over at the beginning of a shot.
